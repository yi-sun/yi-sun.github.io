about

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student in mathematics at MIT, advised by Pavel Etingof. I expect to graduate in June 2016. My CV may be found here.

My research interests are in representation theory and integrable systems and their applications to probability and random matrices. My papers are on my research page.

I am supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Before MIT, I studied mathematics at Cambridge supported by the Churchill Scholarship. I completed my undergraduate studies in mathematics and economics at Harvard, advised by Dennis Gaitsgory.
